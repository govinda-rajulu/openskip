IntroDB (required for skipping)

Get your personal key at introdb.app. Without this, intro/recap/outro skipping is disabled — only resume playback will work.

TMDB (optional)

Free key at themoviedb.org/settings/api. Used to convert TMDB IDs → IMDb IDs for skip segment lookup.

AnimeSkip (optional — anime only)

Uses anime-skip.com as a fallback when IntroDB has no data. A Client ID is required.
