# Get SkipStream

Hey! This was made just for you. Here's how to get it running 💜

## Firefox (Easiest)

Go to https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/skipstream/ and click "Add to Firefox." Done!

## Chrome or Edge

1. Go to github.com/govinda-rajulu/openskip/releases/latest
2. Download the ZIP file
3. Unzip it somewhere safe (like Documents—don't delete it later)
4. Open your browser and go to `chrome://extensions` (or `edge://extensions` for Edge)
5. Turn on "Developer mode" (toggle in the top-right corner)
6. Click "Load unpacked" and select your unzipped folder
7. Done! The puzzle piece icon in your toolbar is SkipStream

## Quick Setup (2 minutes)

1. Click the SkipStream icon in your toolbar
2. Click the ⚙️ gear icon to open Settings
3. Under IntroDB: grab your free key at introdb.app (sign up, copy it, paste it in)
4. That's all you NEED. Everything else is optional.

Optional: Want to sync your watch history across devices? Check the Supabase section in Settings for easy instructions.

## What Does It Do?

- Skips intros, recaps, and outros automatically (instantly in auto mode, or with a 3-second countdown in prompt mode)
- Remembers where you stopped watching, even across devices
- Works on Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, Crunchyroll, and basically any site with video
- Pop open the extension to control playback speed, toggle skipping, or check your watch stats

If something's weird, just let me know 😊
